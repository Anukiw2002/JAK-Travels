Discover Ella

Nature's hidden gem

Ella is a tranquil and picturesque town located in the heart of Sri Lanka's Hill Country. Known for its breathtaking scenery, lush tea plantations, and majestic waterfalls, Ella offers visitors a serene escape from the busyness of everyday life. This charming destination combines natural beauty with opportunities for adventure, making it a must-visit location for travelers.

Best Time to Visit

January - March

Weather

15°C - 25°C

Duration Needed

2-3 Days

Nine Arch Bridge

A magnificent colonial-era railway bridge surrounded by lush tea plantations. This architectural marvel spans 91 meters and stands 24 meters high.

Best Time

6AM - 9AM

Train Times

9:30AM, 3:30PM

Duration

2-3 hours

Visitor Tips:

  • Arrive early for the best photography lighting
  • Check train schedules for perfect photo opportunities
  • Walk along the railway track from Ella station (30 mins)

Ella Rock

A challenging but rewarding hike offering breathtaking panoramic views of Ella Gap and the surrounding hills. The perfect spot for sunrise photography.

Hike Duration

4-5 hours

Difficulty

Moderate

Best Time

Start at 3AM

Hiking Tips:

  • Start early to catch the sunrise
  • Bring plenty of water and snacks
  • Wear proper hiking shoes
  • Consider hiring a local guide

Ravana Falls

A majestic cascading waterfall with mythological significance, perfect for photography and refreshing breaks during your Ella journey.

Height

25 meters

Best Season

Nov - Mar

Duration

1-2 hours

Visitor Tips:

  • Visit early morning to avoid crowds
  • Be careful on slippery rocks
  • Bring a change of clothes if planning to swim

Ella Gap

A breathtaking mountain pass offering panoramic views of the southern plains through a deep valley between the hills. Famous for its spectacular sunrise and sunset views.

Elevation

1,041 meters

Best Time

Early morning

Duration

1-3 hours

Visitor Tips:

  • Visit at sunrise for the clearest views
  • Bring warm clothing as it can be chilly
  • Don't forget your camera

Mini Adam's Peak

A popular hiking destination offering 360-degree views of Ella's landscapes. Named after the sacred Adam's Peak, this smaller version provides a more accessible hiking experience.

Height

1,141 meters

Hike Duration

2-3 hours

Difficulty

Moderate

Visitor Tips:

  • Start early to avoid afternoon heat
  • Wear proper hiking shoes
  • Carry water and snacks

Ravana Cave

A historic cave system associated with the legendary King Ravana. According to the Ramayana, it's believed to be where Princess Sita was hidden during her captivity.

Cave Depth

50 meters

Visit Duration

30-45 mins

Accessibility

Moderate

Visitor Tips:

  • Bring a flashlight
  • Wear sturdy shoes
  • Visit with a guide recommended

Ravana Mega Zip Line

An exciting adventure activity offering breathtaking aerial views of Ella's landscape. One of the longest zip lines in Sri Lanka, stretching across the valley.

Length

500 meters

Duration

15-20 mins

Height

80 meters

Visitor Tips:

  • Book in advance
  • Weight limit applies
  • Follow safety instructions carefully

Ella Swing

A popular photo spot offering a unique experience of swinging over Ella's beautiful valley. Located at various guesthouses and viewpoints around Ella.

Best Time

Morning

Duration

15-30 mins

Cost

Varies

Visitor Tips:

  • Book time slot in advance
  • Wear comfortable clothing
  • Bring camera for photos
