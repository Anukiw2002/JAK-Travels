Experience Ella

Where misty mountains meet colonial charm

Nestled in the misty mountains of Sri Lanka's Hill Country at an elevation of 1,041 meters, Ella is a captivating mountain village that enchants visitors with its serene beauty. Famous for its iconic Nine Arch Bridge, challenging hiking trails, and sprawling tea plantations, this picturesque destination offers a perfect blend of adventure and tranquility. Surrounded by rolling hills shrouded in morning mist, cascading waterfalls, and endless views of tea-covered slopes, Ella provides a peaceful retreat where travelers can immerse themselves in nature while enjoying the cool climate and warm hospitality of the highlands.

Best Time to Visit

January - March

Weather

15°C - 25°C

Duration Needed

2-3 Days

Nine Arch Bridge

A magnificent colonial-era railway bridge surrounded by lush tea plantations. This architectural marvel spans 91 meters and stands 24 meters high.

Best Time

6AM - 9AM

Train Times

9:30AM, 3:30PM

Duration

2-3 hours

Visitor Tips:

  • Arrive early for the best photography lighting
  • Check train schedules for perfect photo opportunities
  • Walk along the railway track from Ella station (30 mins)

Ella Rock

A challenging but rewarding hike offering breathtaking panoramic views of Ella Gap and the surrounding hills. The perfect spot for sunrise photography.

Hike Duration

4-5 hours

Difficulty

Moderate

Best Time

Start at 3AM

Hiking Tips:

  • Start early to catch the sunrise
  • Bring plenty of water and snacks
  • Wear proper hiking shoes
  • Consider hiring a local guide

Ravana Falls

A majestic cascading waterfall with mythological significance, perfect for photography and refreshing breaks during your Ella journey.

Height

25 meters

Best Season

Nov - Mar

Duration

1-2 hours

Visitor Tips:

  • Visit early morning to avoid crowds
  • Be careful on slippery rocks
  • Bring a change of clothes if planning to swim
